Eco-ego

The aliens terraformed Earth

to invigorate its rebirth.

The aliens reduced humanity

to nothing more than a comedy.

The aliens think humanity caused its own demise,

never examining its complacent guise.

This is a humorous yet tragic take

on the trajectory of humanity's fate.
