Beyond The Boundaries

Nostalgia needs no memory

to unlock the chains of past.

Mindfulness lost its mind

to rescue the key of present.

Uncertainty abandoned control

to disperse the mist of future.

I forsaken time as I transcend

beyond the boundaries.

Beyond Comprehension

The ash tree creaks its lullaby,

with roots holding earth and sky.

Gently in my slumber,

I bestowed gifts of gold,

awaiting worlds to unfold.

But once left opened,

with ashes astray,

nothing gold can stay.
