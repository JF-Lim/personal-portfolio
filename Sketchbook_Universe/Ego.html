Qualia

At dawn, a mist of coffee cloaks the air.

At day, a whirl of traffic shrouds the lane.

At dusk, a blur of lights left half-extinguished.

The extras - faint and out of focus.

Possessing qualia

vast and boundless as my own.

Brief moments brimmed with sonder.

What's it like? I wonder.

Unlimited Wants

Abundance creates a world of unlimited wants.

Possessing a crushing weight of desires,

an infinite abyss was born within me.

That which consumes all light

with the pull of its gravity.

If resources are limited,

unlimited wants only

create deficiency.
