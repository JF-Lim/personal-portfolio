We Live In A Society

We live in a society that worships data.

Under the veil of bias,

prejudice and subjectivity,

the quantitative restricts our self-worth

to mere numbers and statistics.

Will we ever realise that

metrics should be

our servants, not our master?

Face The Music

It speaks of white silence;

the epidemic of aggressive loneliness.

It speaks of incorrigible sorrows;

the plague of omnipotent absurdity.

It speaks of unresolved longings;

the torment of forced exile.

If only the Lost Dreamers listened,

but they were too beguiled by the music.

The Weight of Words

The instant it impresses a tint of print,

it either lifts us with a flight of lights,

or burdens us with a spate of weights.

In its latent, light and lifeless wake,

let not the apparition of thought

conquer the mind it sought.
