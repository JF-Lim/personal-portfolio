Genesis

Flames engulfed the oblivion white, dazzling the world of sight.

Ashes seared the cosmos black, cleansing the world of lack.

Tints obliterated flame and flux, invigorating the genesis crux.

As voids of time reverse in flow, the perfect dream recurs in woe.

Renewal

As my chapters wash ashore,

the slate turns blank once more.

Even as my words burn and wither,

they yearn to stand and deliver.

Lost are they in sight and sound,

but in silence, they are found.

There I stood by the sea in awe,

absorbing the hues, suffusing all.
