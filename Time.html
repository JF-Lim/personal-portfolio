Time

Merry Go Round

Through all moments

- past, present, future -

the parallel whispers reverberated infinitely.

As ancient Time sneered conceitedly

at foolish Eternity, forever laughing.

Left only a constant,

with no change nor causality.

Yet the Merry Go Round spins.

The Eternal Spiral

Time is not an arrow.

Where is the illusion of tomorrow?

Time is not a spinning top.

When would it come to a stop?

Time can be only an eternal spiral.

Each juncture intertwines in chiral,

almost like a tempest going viral.

Scorching Wasteland

To derelict dunes of sempiternal sadness,

twisted time only spiraled into

midsummer madness.

To scorched souls

of grosteque glass,

delusional dreams

almost never lavishly last.

To whimsical waters of a grim goodbye,

the reticent rose turned a blind eye.

Blind Eye

To walk a life of chains, the stains

of ash, the world's remains.

The eye was on the blink, the brink

of death, the world shrinks.

Few left to watch our sins, the kings

of vice, the world begins.
